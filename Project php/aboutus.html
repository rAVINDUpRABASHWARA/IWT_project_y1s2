The story of Frandy Music

The Frandy Music Centre began its illustrious journey way back in 2000. when our leader introduced Frandy guitar to the island. This makes us the oldest distributor in the world for Frandy, outside Sri lanka. This amplified the scope of the business by offering a much wider range of products. This included everything from musical instruments to home audio, accessories, expertise and much more. The organization moreover incorporated a fully-fledged service center, which today is renowned as one of the finest state-of-the-art service centers on the island. The Frandy flagship store is also supported by an island-wide dealer network with over 10 outlets and growing.
